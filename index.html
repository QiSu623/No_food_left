About No Food Left

Every day there are lots of activities on campus. After those activities, lots of food left, which is a huge waste.
In order to save food and help students and staff save money when they are on campus. The No Food Left website wants to be the bridge between the activity organizer and students. The organizer can post the information when there is food left after the activity on campus. Students and staff can browse those posts to register that activity. After that, they can go to the location and enjoy the food!
